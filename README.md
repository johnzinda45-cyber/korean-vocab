A fully offline-capable Korean vocabulary learning app.  
  
-----  
  
## Option 1 — Use as a Local File (No Internet Needed)  
  
1. Open `index.html` in any browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)  
1. Your words save automatically via `localStorage`  
1. Works 100% offline after first open  
  
-----  
  
## Option 2 — Deploy Free Online (Netlify — Easiest)  
  
1. Go to https://app.netlify.com  
1. Sign up free (use GitHub, Google, or email)  
1. Drag the entire **korean-vocab-app** folder onto the Netlify dashboard  
1. You’ll get a free URL like `https://your-app-name.netlify.app`  
1. Share this URL or bookmark it on your phone  
  
-----  
  
## Option 3 — Deploy Free Online (Vercel)  
  
1. Install Node.js from https://nodejs.org  
1. Run in terminal:  
     
   ```  
   npm i -g vercel  
   cd korean-vocab-app  
   vercel  
   ```  
1. Follow the prompts — you’ll get a free URL  
  
-----  
  
## Option 4 — Install on Your Phone (PWA)  
  
After deploying online (Option 2 or 3):  
  
**Android (Chrome):**  
  
- Open the URL in Chrome  
- Tap the “Install” banner that appears at the bottom, OR  
- Tap the 3-dot menu → “Add to Home Screen”  
  
**iPhone (Safari):**  
  
- Open the URL in Safari  
- Tap the Share button (box with arrow) → “Add to Home Screen”  
- Tap “Add”  
  
The app will appear on your home screen and work offline!  
  
-----  
  
## Your Data  
  
- All vocabulary is saved in your browser’s localStorage  
- Data persists between sessions on the same device/browser  
- To transfer data to another device: use the browser’s export/sync features
